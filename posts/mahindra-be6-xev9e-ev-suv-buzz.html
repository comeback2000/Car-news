Mahindra BE 6 and XEV 9e Are Turning India's EV SUV Race Into a Real Fight

By Car News Desk | June 3, 2026

Trending topics: Mahindra BE 6, XEV 9e, born-electric SUVs, India EV sales.

Why it matters

Mahindra's born-electric SUVs are no longer just launch-stage drama. Autocar India's May 2026 EV sales roundup placed Mahindra second among EV makers with 6,133 units, a sign that BE 6 and XEV 9e interest is turning into measurable showroom momentum.

X trend discovery around Mahindra's electric push showed the same models anchoring the conversation: BE 6 for its aggressive coupe-SUV look and XEV 9e for buyers who want a larger electric family SUV. That combination gives Mahindra something most EV rivals want: both style-led buzz and practical SUV credibility.

The key question is whether Mahindra can sustain deliveries as India EV shoppers compare real range, software, charging experience and service support. If it can, the BE 6/XEV 9e pair could become the first serious homegrown challenge to Tata's long EV lead.

Quick hits

  • Mahindra recorded 6,133 EV sales in May 2026, according to Autocar India.
  • BE 6 and XEV 9e are drawing attention for design, range claims and cabin tech.
  • Public X trend results highlighted Mahindra's electric SUV strategy as a recurring India auto topic.
