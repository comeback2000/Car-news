New MG Ev Model: What readers should know

JSW MG Motor is preparing a comprehensive product offensive, with a new-generation Astor and a replacement for the ZS EV on the cards, according to sources. The development comes as the MG Windsor EV stormed to the top of India's EV sales charts in 2025, recording 46,735 units and beating the Tata Nexon EV. Windsor EV: The New Sales Champion The MG Windsor EV has overtaken the Tata Nexon EV to become India's best-selling electric car in 2025. With 46,735 units sold, the Windsor has carved a significant lead, driven by its spacious design, competitive pricing, and the innovative Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS) model that lowers the upfront cost. The BaaS offering, which separates the battery cost from the car price, has been a key differentiator for MG, making EVs more accessible to cost-conscious buyers. New Astor and ZS EV Replacement Autocar India reports that JSW MG Motor is readying a new Astor and a replacement for the ZS EV. The new Astor is expected to receive a comprehensive update with fresh styling, upgraded features, and possibly new powertrain options. Meanwhile, the ZS EV replacement will likely be a ground-up new model, moving beyond the current generation's platform to offer better range, performance, and technology. This aligns with MG's strategy to strengthen its EV portfolio and compete with upcoming launches from Tata, Maruti Suzuki, and Hyundai. BaaS Pricing Dominance CarDekho notes that MG dominates the list of EVs with Battery-as-a-Service pricing in India. The Windsor EV's BaaS variant starts at an aggressive price point, undercutting many rivals. New Tata and Maruti EVs are also adopting similar models, but MG's early mover advantage and strong dealer network have helped it maintain leadership. For buyers, BaaS reduces the initial purchase price by ₹1.5-2 lakh, making EVs more affordable, though monthly battery rental fees apply. Market Context and Buyer Takeaways MG's 2025 success underscores the growing appetite for EVs in India, especially in the sub-₹15 lakh segment. The Windsor EV's spacious cabin and practical range (up to 330 km) have resonated with families. For buyers considering an EV, the Windsor offers strong value, especially with BaaS. However, the upcoming ZS EV replacement could bring even better range and features, so those not in a hurry may want to wait. The new Astor, meanwhile, will continue to target the compact SUV segment with petrol and possibly hybrid options. Conclusion JSW MG Motor is on a roll. The Windsor EV's sales leadership and the imminent arrival of a new Astor and ZS EV replacement signal the company's commitment to both ICE and electric lineups. For Indian car buyers, this means more choice, better technology, and competitive pricing in the months ahead. Frequently Asked Questions What is the Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS) model? BaaS allows customers to buy the car without the battery, paying a monthly rental for the battery. This reduces the upfront cost significantly. When will the new MG Astor launch? Yes, if you need a spacious, practical EV with good range and want to save on upfront cost via BaaS. However, if you can wait, the ZS EV replacement may offer more advanced tech.
