2027 Toyota GR86 Gets Sharper: New Thunder Color, Better Shifts, Same 228-HP Fun

By Car News Desk | June 3, 2026

Trending keywords: Toyota GR86, 2027 Toyota GR86, sports car, manual transmission.

Why it matters

Toyota's GR86 remains one of the few attainable rear-wheel-drive sports cars on sale, and the 2027 update keeps the formula focused. Toyota says the coupe gets revised throttle calibration, improved shift feel, new cabin treatments, and expanded convenience and safety features.

The headline visual change is a new Thunder exterior color, joined by an available Cockpit Red interior on Premium grades. The performance recipe stays familiar: a 2.4-liter boxer four-cylinder rated at 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, with manual and automatic transmissions offered.

The 2027 GR86 is expected to arrive at Toyota dealerships in summer 2026, with pricing and final specs coming later.

Quick hits

  • New throttle tuning aims for smoother, more linear response.
  • Toyota says fifth-to-fourth downshift feel has been improved.
  • An available Performance Package includes Brembo brakes and SACHS dampers.

Source: Toyota USA Newsroom
